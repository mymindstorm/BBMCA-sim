Margolus BBMCA Simulator

How to play: Click and drag on the grid to draw. Use the brush selector to place particles, blocks, plan routes with the path marker, or erase mistakes.

Rules (Margolus 1984): Single particles move diagonally. Head-on diagonal collisions rotate 90 degrees. All other configurations (sum of 0, 3, 4, or 2 adjacent) remain unchanged.
